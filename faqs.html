Frequently Asked Questions

General

Here you’ll find answers to the most common questions we receive. Feel free to browse the sections below for more information.

You can make a reservation through our website by going to the 'Reservations' page and filling out the form. Once completed, you’ll receive a confirmation email with the details.

To cancel or modify your reservation, log into your account and navigate to your reservation details. You can then choose to cancel or make changes. Please note that changes may be subject to availability.

Yes, we do accept walk-ins based on availability. However, we recommend making a reservation in advance to ensure your preferred time and date.

If you need to reset your password, go to the login page and click on 'Forgot Password?' Follow the instructions sent to your email to create a new password.

If you are having trouble logging in, ensure that you are entering the correct email and password. If you still cannot log in, try resetting your password or contact our support team for assistance.
